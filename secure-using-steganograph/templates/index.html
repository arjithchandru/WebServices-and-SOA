This web app embed your message into a png image, and makes your message undetectable for a normal human and for many machines!!!

Things to keep in mind:

i) Please use it just in fun purpose. Don't make misuse of it!

ii) Always use .png images (other formats are not supported).

iii) Try to avoid black image, it may misbehave. Try to use colorful image.

steps to encode:

i) Go to encode, then upload your png image (the image where you want to hide your message). then put your message in the text bar and press submit

ii) It will take you into a page where have to download the encoded png image copy the pixel length.

iii) If you want your friend to get the hidden image, give them the original as well as the encoded image. Don't forget the pixel length! or it won't work.

steps to decode:

i) First upload the original image and the encoded image. then input the correct pixel length and press submit.

ii) voilà!!! here you have your  hidden message!!!
